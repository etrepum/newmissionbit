Out of Site

Fall 2013 Semester Program Partner

Out of Site is a non-profit that provides free after school and summer programming in visual and performing arts to public high school students in San Francisco.

Mission Bit and Out of Site partnered to offer the Mission Bit 'intro to computer programming' class during the Fall 2013 semester.

The collaboration was a tremendous success. The class was over subscribed by more than 200% and the drop rate was 0%. Out of Site of Mission Bit intend to build on the partnership success for the Spring semester.
