Increasing Reach

Mission Bit's 'intro to computer programming' class creates an opportunity for 'net new' students to explore computer programming as a potential academic and professional pursuit.

Accelerating Education

Mission Bit classes provides an opportunity for students planning to study and/or work in computer science to develop a base understanding of CS principles before taking a CS class offered at their school.

Workforce Skill Development

Mission Bit classes equip public school students studying CS in school with complementary knowledge and Skillls that increases their preparedness for professional opportunities.

Out of Site. Featured Program Partner

Out of Site is a co-producer of Mission Bit classes. Out of Site is a non-profit that provides free after school and summer programming in visual and performing arts to public high school students in San Francisco.
Out of Site programs fill a gap for teenagers – not only are they not getting enough art in their schools but there are few safe, free, fun and challenging programs for teens. Students come to Out of Site looking for the chance to experiment in the arts, and for a place to be themselves-they find artistic training, new mediums for self-expression, and a diverse and supportive community.
